Our Story

Started as a family business with a team of 2 back in 2009, we now have a team of 10, all loving cakes, of course!

Local + Enviromently Friendly

We source 70% of our ingredients from suppliers within 30 miles radius, and get the rest from Fairtrade business partners. All our packaging is made off 100% recyclable cardboard
